ABOUT

This Black Womxn Businesses Map is an interactive map that highlights Black womxn-owned businesses.

According to a 2019 study by American Express, Black Womxn-owned businesses are the second largest segment of all womxn-owned businesses, yet they earn the least of any minority. It's our mission as a group of caring humans, to impact that revenue gap by providing and maintaining this map so that we can continue to support our favorite restaurants, bars, retail shops, cafes, and much more. We hope you'll utilize this map and share it widely!

Our work doesn’t end here. We understand that this list is by no means exhaustive, we’d like to continue this effort and even expand outside of home in NYC with your help. Please join us in this endeavor and share any and all Black womxn-owned businesses in NYC and beyond, because where we spend our money matters.
